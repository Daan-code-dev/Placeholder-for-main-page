Improving Chess – Chess Book Reviews and Improvement Guides

Hi, I'm a ~2000 rated tournament player who's spent years figuring out what actually works to improve at chess — and what's a waste of time. On this site, you'll find honest book reviews and practical guides, based on real experience rather than marketing claims, to help you climb the rating ladder faster.

Improve your tactics

As a 2000-rated tournament player, I know how important tactics are. Almost every game is decided by tactical opportunities such as pins, forks, and skewers. Visit this page if you want to improve your tactical vision and become a stronger player.

Improve as a beginner

I know that improving at chess as a beginner can be difficult. You may not know where to start, and because of your limited experience, it can be hard to tell whether you're training the right way. This page is designed to help beginners improve their chess and build a solid foundation for future progress.
